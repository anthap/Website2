About

Hi, I'm Anthony! I am University of Waterloo graduate with a degree in Mechatronics Engineering. My previous experiences range from wearable tech design to software automation in a software development firm to mechanical design at a medical device company proving I am a flexible and quick learner.

I have a passion for technology specifically with an interest in electro-mechanical design. I am a creative thinker who enjoys solving real world problems and I am always eager to learn more.
